Tribute to Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein (1879-1955) was a German-born theoretical physicist who developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics (alongside quantum mechanics). He is best known for the equation E=mc², which expresses the equivalence of mass and energy. His work revolutionized our understanding of space, time, and gravity.

Famous Quotes

  • "Imagination is more important than knowledge." – Albert Einstein
  • "Life is like riding a bicycle. To keep your balance, you must keep moving." – Albert Einstein
  • "The important thing is not to stop questioning. Curiosity has its own reason for existing." – Albert Einstein
